★☆☆   hexose
 / ˈheksəus /◙ noun

【语源】1. late 19th cent.: from hexa- 'six' + -ose

★☆☆   hey
 / heɪ /◙ exclamation
1. used to attract attention, to express surprise, interest, or annoyance, or to elicit agreement  • （用于吸引注意力，表示惊讶、兴趣或恼怒，或诱使同意）嘿，喂

【IDIOMS】◘ hey up

◘ what the hey
