 / ˈaisəˌspin /◙ noun
1. [mass noun] [Physics] a vector quantity or quantum number assigned to subatomic particles and atomic nuclei and having values such that similar particles differing only in charge-related properties (independent of the strong interaction between particles) can be treated as different states of a single particle  • [物理] 同位旋

【语源】1. 1960s: contraction of isotopic spin, isobaric spin

★☆☆   isostasy
 / aiˈsɔstəsi /◙ noun
1. [mass noun] [Geology] the equilibrium that exists between parts of the earth's crust, which behaves as if it consists of blocks floating on the underlying mantle, rising if material (such as an ice cap) is removed and sinking if material is deposited  • [地质] 地壳均衡

【派生】♦ isostatic adjective

【语源】1. late 19th cent.: from iso- 'equal' + Greek stasis 'station'

★☆☆   isotactic
 / ˌaisəuˈtæktik /◙ adjective
1. [Chemistry] (of a polymer) in which all the repeating units have the same stereochemical configuration  • [化] （聚合体）全同立构的

【语源】1. 1950s: from iso- 'equal' + Greek taktos 'arranged' + -ic

★☆☆   isothere
 / ˈaisəuˌθiə /◙ noun
1. [Meteorology] a line on a map connecting points having the same average temperature in summer  • [气象] 等夏温线

【语源】1. mid 19th cent.: from French isothère, from Greek isos 'equal' + theros 'summer'

★☆☆   isotherm
 / ˈaisəuθə:m /◙ noun
1. a line on a map connecting points having the same temperature at a given time or on average over a given period  • 恒温线
  [Physics] a curve on a diagram joining points representing states or conditions of equal temperature  • [物理] 等温线

【派生】♦ isothermal adjective & noun
♦ isothermally adverb

【语源】1. mid 19th cent.: from French isotherme, from Greek isos 'equal' + thermē 'heat'

★☆☆   isotonic
 / ˌaisəuˈtɔnik /◙ adjective
1. [Physiology] (of muscle action) taking place with normal contraction  • [生理] （肌肉活动）等张的
2. [Physiology] denoting or relating to a solution having the same osmotic pressure as some other solution, especially one in a cell or a body fluid  • [生理] 等渗压的
  (of a drink) containing essential salts and minerals in the same concentration as in the body and intended to replace those lost as a result of sweating during vigorous exercise  • （饮料）含（与体内相同浓度的）基本盐分和矿物质的（以补充身体剧烈运动出汗后流失的盐分和矿物质）

【派生】♦ isotonically adverb
♦ isotonicity noun

【语源】1. early 19th cent. (as a musical term designating a system of tuning, characterized by equal intervals): from Greek isotonos, from isos 'equal' + tonos 'tone'

★☆☆   isotope
 / ˈaɪsəˌtəʊp /◙ noun
1. [Chemistry] each of two or more forms of the same element that contain equal numbers of protons but different numbers of neutrons in their nuclei, and hence differ in relative atomic mass but not in chemical properties; in particular, a radioactive form of an element  • [化] 同位素

【派生】♦ isotopic adjective
♦ isotopically adverb
♦ isotopy noun

【语源】1. 1913: coined by F. Soddy, from iso- 'equal' + Greek topos 'place' (because the isotopes occupy the same place in the periodic table of elements)
