【派生】♦ millennial adjective

【语源】1. mid 17th cent.: modern Latin, from Latin mille 'thousand', on the pattern of biennium

【用法】1. The correct spelling is millennium not millenium. The latter is a common error (found in just over 10 per cent of citations in the British National Corpus), formed by analogy with other similar words correctly spelled with only one n, such as millenarian and millenary. The differences in spelling are explained by different origins. Millennium was formed by analogy with words like biennium, while millenary and millenarian were formed on the Latin milleni
