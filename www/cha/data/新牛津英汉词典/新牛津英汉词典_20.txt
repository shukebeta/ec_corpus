1. a flat strip of land, raised bank, or terrace bordering a river or canal  • 沿河狭长平地（或沿河高岸、沿河阶地）
  a path or grass strip beside a road  • 大路旁边的小径（或草径），路肩
  an artificial ridge or embankment, such as one built as a defence against tanks  • （反坦克工事等的）人工脊突，护堤，护坡道
  a narrow space, especially one between a ditch and the base of a parapet  • （尤指沟渠与护墙墙脚之间的）狭窄空地

【语源】1. early 18th cent. (denoting a narrow space): from French berme, from Dutch berm
