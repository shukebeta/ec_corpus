【用法】1. The construction centre round or centre around (as opposed to center on) has been denounced as incorrect since it first appeared in the middle of the 19th century. 【用法】1. 1 Strictly speaking, centuries run from 01 to 100, meaning that the new century begins on the first day of the year 01 (i.e. 1 January 1901, 1 January 2001, etc.). Thus, 1066 is a date in the 11th century, 1542 is a date in the 16th century, and so on
