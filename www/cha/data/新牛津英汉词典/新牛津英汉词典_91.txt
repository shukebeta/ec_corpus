1. the paired respiratory organ of fishes and some amphibians, by which oxygen is extracted from water flowing over surfaces within or attached to the walls of the pharynx  • 鳃（鱼和一些两栖动物的呼吸器官）
  an organ of similar function in an invertebrate animal  • （无脊椎动物）鳃状呼吸器官
2. the vertical plates arranged radially on the underside of mushrooms and many toadstools  • （蘑菇和多种伞菌的）菌褶
3. the wattles or dewlap of a fowl  • （家禽）下腭垂肉
  (gills)the flesh below a person's jaws and ears  • 腮下肉
 »we stuffed ourselves to the gills with scrambled eggs on toast.  摊鸡蛋加吐司吃得我们腮帮子鼓鼓的。
◙ verb, [with obj.] 
1. gut or clean (a fish)  • 取出（鱼的）内脏；洗鱼
2. catch (a fish) in a gill net  • （用刺网）捕鱼
