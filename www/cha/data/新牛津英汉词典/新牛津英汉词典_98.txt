 »[as adv.] he stopped halfway down the passage.  他在通道上走了一半停下来。
 »[as adj.] during the night we passed Kingoonya, the halfway mark.  夜里我们经过了位于中途的金古尼亚。
  in the middle of a period of time  • （时间）到一半

  [as adv.] to some extent  • 一定程度上；部分地

★☆☆   halibut
 / ˈhæləbət , ˈhɔl- /◙ [复同单] 
1. a northern marine fish which is the largest of the flatfishes and important as a food fish  • 庸鲽，大比目鱼。
--› see Greenland halibut 
2. Genus Hippoglossus, family Pleuronectidae: H. hippoglossus of the Atlantic and H. stenolepis of the Pacific  • 庸鲽属，鲽科：大西洋的庸鲽和太平洋的狭鳞庸鲽
--› see Greenland halibut 
  used in names of large edible flatfishes of other families, e.g. Queensland halibut  • [用于其他科的可食用的大型比目鱼的名称中，例如Queensland halibut] 

【语源】1. late Middle English: from haly 'holy' + obsolete butt 'flatfish' (because it was often eaten on holy days)
