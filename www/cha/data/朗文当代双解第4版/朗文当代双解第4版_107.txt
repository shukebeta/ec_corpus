I need to clean (NOT I need clean) the house.  我得打扫一下房子了。
If someone else is going to do something for you, you can say that you need something done or need something doing . 如果别人要为你做某事，你可以说 need something done 或 need something doing: 
I need my car fixed urgently.  我的车急需修理。

When you are talking about the object that is going to have something done to it, you can say that it needs something doing or needs something to be done . 当说到需要对某物做某事时，可以说 it needs something doing 或 needs something to be done:

That box needs to be moved (NOT needs moved).  那个箱子需要挪一下。
Negatives 否定句You can say that you don't need to do something or needn't do something. 可以说 don't need to do something 或 needn't do something: 
I don't need to leave (NOT don't need leave) until 10.  我不必在 10 点以前走。

 ► need not means that it is not necessary to do something. Do not use it to mean must not (=are not allowed to). need not 的意思是“不必”做某事，不要用它来表示 must not（不准）的意思: 
You needn't take any money.  你不必带钱。

 ► needn't have means that it was not necessary for someone to do something that they in fact did. Do not use it to mean didn't need to or didn't have to (=something was not necessary). need't have 意为某人“本不该”做某事，不要用它来表达 didn't need to 或 didn't have to（不必）的意思: 
We needn't have ordered so much food.  我们本不该点这么多吃的。
I didn't need OR didn't have to tell him who I was – he already knew.  我没有必要告诉他我是谁 — 他已经知道了。
