 GRAMMAR 
The verb pay is followed directly by a noun when you are talking about paying a person. 如果想要表达把钱支付给某人，pay 后面直接跟名词: 
I'll pay you tomorrow.  明天我付钱给你。
I haven't paid my accountant yet.  我还没付钱给我的会计师呢。
Pay is also followed directly by a noun when you are talking about the amount of money you pay. 如果想要表达所支付的钱数，pay 后面也直接跟名词: 
I've already paid ￡700.  我已支付了 700 英镑。
 ► Do not use pay followed directly by a noun referring to the thing you are buying. Use pay (an amount of money) for something. pay 后面紧跟的名词不是所买的东西，用 pay（多少钱）for something: 
When I paid for my tickets (NOT paid my tickets) the man told me there was no discount.  我付钱买票的时候，那个男人告诉我没有折扣。
I paid ￡100 for this jacket.  我买这件短外套花了 100 英镑。
When you are talking about whether you pay for something using a cheque, a credit card etc, use pay by . 用支票、信用卡等支付的时候，要用 pay by: 
If you pay by credit card, you get free insurance.  如果用信用卡支付，可以获得免费保险。
When you are talking about the type of money you use to pay something, use pay in. 当说起买东西使用的货币种类时，用 pay in: 
You can only pay in euros.  你只能付欧元。
