 ► Reason is never followed by of or because . reason 后面从来不跟 of 或 because。 
You can talk about the reason for something, the reason that something happens, or the reason why something happens. 表示某事的原因可以说 reason for， reason that 或 reason why:

I like children, and that's the reason I became a teacher.  我喜欢孩子，这是我当老师的原因。
