type, kind, sort:
 Type, kind, and sort all have the same meaning and can be used in the same situations. type, kind 和 sort 意思相同，可用于相同的情况下: 
What type of car do you drive?  你开的是什么类型的车？

   If you are saying that something is partly true or are not being exact, use sort of or kind of rather than type of .  如果说某事部分那样或不完全那样时，用 sort of 或 kind of，而不用 type of:

Type, kind, and sort are countable nouns, and they must be plural after determiners with plural meanings. type, kind 和 sort 都是可数名词，在具有复数含义的限定词后必须用复数形式: 
 »this type / kind / sort of + singular noun :

Red wine goes well with this sort of dish.  红葡萄酒配这种菜非常合适。
 »these / those types / kinds / sorts of + plural / singular noun : 
How common are these types of illness(es)?  这些类型的疾病有多常见？
Those kinds of colours look good with dark skin.  那些颜色配深色皮肤很好看。
