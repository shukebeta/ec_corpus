 CHOICE 
under, underneath, below, beneath:
   Under is the usual way to say that one thing is at a lower level than another, or is covered by it.  under 是表示某物在另一物体的下面或底下的一种常用说法:
Your shoes are under the table.  你的鞋子在桌子底下。
He lay under a blanket.  他盖着一条毛毯躺着。
▪ Under is nearly always used as a preposition (followed by a noun). under  几乎总是用作介词，后接名词。
Underneath has a similar meaning to under, and is used especially when something is hidden or covered. underneath 和 under 的意思接近，尤用于表示某物被藏在下面或盖在下面: 
a box underneath the floorboards  藏在地板下面的盒子
   It can also be used as an adverb.  underneath 也可用作副词:
She lifted the cover and peeped underneath.  她掀起盖子朝下面瞥了一眼。
   Below is used when one thing is at a much lower level than another, and can be a preposition or an adverb.  below 表示某物比另一物的位置要低得多，可用作介词，也可用作副词:
the apartment below ours  我们楼下的公寓
Mist lay in the valley below.  下面的山谷里薄雾弥漫。
Beneath can be used in the same ways as under and below, but is a more literary or formal word. beneath 的使用方法与 under 和 below 相同，不过这个单词更富文学性，更正式: 
beneath the silvery moon  在银色的月光下
the cliff, and the ocean beneath  悬崖和下面的大海
