 CHOICE 
call, phone, telephone, ring:
   In spoken English, it is usual to say that you call or phone someone.  英语口语中一般都说 call someone 或 phone someone（打电话给某人）:
He calls me almost every day.  他几乎每天给我打电话。

   In spoken British English, it is also very usual to say that you ring someone.  在英国英语的口语里，一般也说 ring someone:

   It is fairly formal and not very usual in spoken English to say that you telephone someone.  在英语口语中，说 telephone someone 相当正式，不是很常见。
 ► Do not say that you 'call to' someone. 不要说 call to someone:
I called him (NOT called to him) to let him know.  我打电话给他让他知道。

Give me a call sometime.  有空给我来电话。
