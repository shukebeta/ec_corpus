★☆☆   during
   dur·ing / ˈdurɪŋ ;  ˈdjʊərɪŋ / 
→preposition1. from the beginning to the end of a period of time  在… 期间:

 CHOICE 
during, while:
 ► Do not say 不要说 'during doing something'. Say 而要说 while doing something: 
While travelling to work, I saw an accident (NOT During travelling to work, I saw an accident).  在上班的路上我看到了一起交通事故。
 ► Do not say 不要说 'during someone does something'. Say 而要说 while someone does something: 
He stole her money while she slept (NOT He stole her money during she slept).  他趁她睡觉时偷了她的钱。
 ► Do not say 'during someone is young / asleep etc'. Say while someone is young / asleep etc . 不要说 during someone is young / asleep etc，而要说 while someone is young / asleep etc: 
It's best to get your teeth fixed while you're still young (NOT during you're still young).  最好是趁还年轻时把你的牙齿弄好。
during, for, or since? 用 during, for 还是用 since？ See note at 见 SINCE 词语辨析

 CHOICE 
each, every It is often correct to use either each or every, but they have slightly different meanings. 使用 each 或 every 常常都是正确的，但意思上略有区别。
