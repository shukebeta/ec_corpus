 GRAMMAR 
enough comes after adjectives, never before. enough 用于形容词之后，不用于形容词之前: 
This one is big enough (NOT enough big).  这一个够大了。
Is it warm enough for you?  你觉得够暖和吗？
enough usually comes before nouns. enough 一般用在名词之前: 
We haven't got enough time.  我们没有足够的时间。
Are there enough plates?  盘子够吗？
Sometimes people use enough after nouns in particular expressions, but this is not very usual. 有时候人们在特定的表达法中把 enough 用在名词之后，但这并不常见: 
There will be time enough for that later.  以后会有足够的时间做那件事。
 ► Nouns used with enough must be plural or uncountable, never singular. 与 enough 连用的名词必须是复数或不可数名词，从来不用单数: 
There aren't enough books (NOT enough book).  书不够。
She has enough money to live on.  她有足够的生活费。
