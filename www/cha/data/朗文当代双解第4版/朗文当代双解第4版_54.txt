 CHOICE 
especially, specially:
   It is better to use especially in front of adjectives to emphasize them, although some people also use specially .  在形容词前面最好用 especially 表示强调，虽然也有人使用 specially:
The cake was especially good.  那蛋糕特别好吃。

   Use especially to say that something applies more to one thing or situation than to others.  especially 用于表示某事物相比之下更适用于某一事物或某一情况:
Everyone loved it, especially the children.  人人都喜欢这东西，尤其是孩子们。

   Use specially to say that something is done or made for a particular purpose.  specially 用于表示某事物是为某一特定目的所做或制作的:
I got this specially for you.  这是我特地为你弄来的。
specially designed equipment  专门设计的设备
 ► Especially never comes at the start of a sentence. especially 从来不位于句子的开头:
He loves fruit. He especially likes kiwis (NOT Especially he likes...).  他爱吃水果，尤其喜欢狝猴桃。
