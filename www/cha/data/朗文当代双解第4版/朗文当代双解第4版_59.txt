 CHOICE 
a few , few , a little , little , a bit , fewer , less:
 a few and few are used before plural nouns. a few 和 few 用于复数名词之前。 
   a few means 'a small number'.  a few 的意思是“少数”:
It will take a few minutes.  这要花几分钟时间。
I've got a few friends who live nearby.  我有几个住在附近的朋友。
   few means 'not many'.  It emphasizes how small the number is. It is mainly used in writing or formal speech. few 的意思是“不多”，强调数量之少，主要用于书面或正式讲话中:
   Few people would deny her talent.  几乎没人会否认她的天赋。
He has few inter-ests outside his work.  工作之外他几乎没什么兴趣。
   In spoken English or informal writing it is more usual to say not many .  在英语口语或非正式书面语中更常用的说法是 not many:
Not many people saw what happened.  没多少人目睹事情的经过。
a little and little are used before uncountable nouns. a little 和 little 用于不可数名词之前。 
   a little means 'some, but not a lot'.  a little 的意思是“一些，但是不多”:
We still have a little time left.  我们还有些时间。
   In spoken British English, it is more usual to say a bit .  在英国英语口语中，更常用的说法是 a bit:
'Are you tired?' 'A bit.'  “你累不累？”“有点。”
I've got a bit of money left.  我还剩了点钱。
   little means 'not much'.  It emphasizes how small the amount is. It is mainly used in writing or formal speech. little 的意思是“不多”，强调数量之少，主要用于书面语或正式讲话中:
There is now little hope of success.  眼下成功的希望渺茫。
   In spoken English or informal writing it is more usual to say not much .  在英语口语或非正式书面语中更常用的说法是 not much:
There was not much milk left.  没多少牛奶剩下了。
   The comparative of few is fewer .  few 的比较级是 fewer:
Few people have read the book, and even fewer understand it.  很少有人看过这本书，看懂的人就更少了。
   The comparative of little is less .  little 的比较级是 less:
We know little about his adult life, and less about his childhood.  我们对他的成年生活了解很少，对他的童年知道的就更少了。
 ► Sometimes people use less before a plural noun, but many people think that this is incorrect, so it is bet-ter to use fewer . 有时人们把 less 用在复数名词之前，但是许多人认为这不正确，所以最好用 fewer:
