 GRAMMAR 
Do not use hardly with a negative word. hardly 不和表示否定的词连用: 
I can hardly believe he said that (NOT I can't hardly believe he said that).  我很难相信他说过那样的话。
There's hardly any milk left (NOT There's hardly no milk left).  几乎没剩下多少牛奶。
Use hardly just before the main verb. hardly 用于句子的主要动词之前: 
He could hardly speak (NOT He hardly could speak).  他几乎说不出话来。
Do not use hardly at the beginning of a sentence, except in very formal writing. hardly 不用于句子开头，除非是在非常正式的书面语中:
I had hardly got in the house when the phone rang is the usual way to say this.  （我刚进屋子电话就响了）是通常的说法。
It is possible to say 可以说:
Hardly had I got in the house when the phone rang but this is very formal.  不过这种说法相当正式。
 ► Do not use hardly as the adverb of hard . The adverb of hard is hard . 不要把 hardly 当作 hard 的副词来用。hard 的副词是 hard: 
I tried hard to remember (NOT I tried hardly to remember).  我努力记住。
Students have to study very hard (NOT Students have to study very hardly).  学生们必须努力学习。
