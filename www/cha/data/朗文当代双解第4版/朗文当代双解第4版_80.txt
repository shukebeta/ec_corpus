When you are using if to talk about something that might happen in the future, use the present simple tense, not will or shall . 当用 if 来指未来会发生某事时，其后用一般现在时，不用 will 或 shall:

If someone gave me the money, I'd buy a car tomorrow (NOT If someone would give me the money...).  如果有人给我钱，明天我就买一辆汽车。
 ► In formal English or in writing, use were not was when the subject of the clause is I, he, she, it, there or a singular noun. 正式英语或书面英语中，当句子的主语是 I, he, she, it, there 或单数名词时，要用 were ，而不用 was:

If I was ten years younger, I'd go out with him.  如果我年轻十岁的话，我就和他出去约会了。
