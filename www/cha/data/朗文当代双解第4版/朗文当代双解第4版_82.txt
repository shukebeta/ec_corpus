   include sth in / on sth

 CHOICE 
include, consist of, comprise, be composed of, be made up of:
   Use include to mention only some of the things that something has as its parts.  include（包括）用于指某物中包括的某些部分:
The price includes lunch.  价格里包括午餐。
   If you want to mention all the parts that something has in it, use consist of, comprise, be composed of, or be made up of .  指某物中的所有部分用 consist of, comprise, be composed of 或 be made up of:
The Romance family of languages consists of French, Spanish, Italian, and several other languages.  罗曼语族包括法语、西班牙语、意大利语和其他几种语言。
The house comprises two bedrooms, a kitchen and a living room.  这房子有两间卧室，一间厨房和一间起居室。
The jury was composed of nine whites, one Hispanic, and two Asian Americans.  陪审团由九个白人、一个西班牙裔美国人和两个亚裔美国人组成。
an organization made up of 600,000 small business owners  由 60 万个小企业主构成的组织
 ► Do not say that something 'is consisted of' certain things or that it 'consists' them. Say it consists of them. 不要说 something 'is consisted of' certain things 或 it 'consists' them，而要说 it consists of them。 
 ► Do not say that something 'comprises of' certain things, even though you might hear English speakers say this. Most careful users consider this to be incorrect so you should avoid using it. 不要说 something comprises of certain things，即使你可能听到说英语的人这么说。 大多数讲究的使用者认为这一说法不正确，所以应该避免这么说。
 ► Do not say that something 'is composed by' or 'is composed with' certain things. Say it is composed of them. 不要说 something 'is composed by' 或 'is composed with' certain things，而要说 it is composed of them。
