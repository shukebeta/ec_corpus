, Your job is the work that you do regularly in order to earn money, especially when you work for a company or public organization. job 
   指为赚取工资而固定从事的工作，尤指为公司或公共机构所做的工作:
My last job was with a computer firm.  我上一份工作是在一家计算机公司上班。
He finally got a job in a supermarket.  他最后获得了一份在超市上班的工作。
 Work is used in a more general way to talk about activities that you do to earn money, either working for a company or for yourself. 
   work 的用法更为广泛，指以赚钱为目的、为公司或自己所进行的活动:
Will you go back to work when you've had the baby?  你生了孩子后还会回去工作吗？
I started work when I was 18.  我十八岁开始工作。
 * Do not say 'what is your job?' or 'what is your work?'. Say what do you do? or what do you do for a living? 
   不要说 what is your job? 或 what is your work?，而要说 what do you do? or 或 what do you do for a living?
 Post and position are more formal words for a job in a company or organization. They are used especially in job advertisements and when you are talking about someone moving to a different job. 
   post 和 position 是更正式的说法，指公司或团体中的职位，尤用于招聘广告和谈到某人换工作时:
This post would suit a recent graduate.  这一职位适合刚毕业的大学生。
