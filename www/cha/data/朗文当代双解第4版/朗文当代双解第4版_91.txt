 CHOICE 
lay, lie:
   The verb lay must have an object.  It is a slightly literary way to say 'put something somewhere'. 动词 lay 必须有一个宾语，意为“把某物放到某处”，略带一点文学色彩:

   The verb lie does not have an object.  It means 'be or get into a horizontal position somewhere'. 动词 lie 不带宾语，意为“平躺在某处”:
Let's lie on the grass.  我们来躺在草地上吧。
Lie down here for a while.  在这里躺一会。
