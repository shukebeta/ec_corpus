1.PHRASAL VERB	短语动词  （用谎话或借口）欺骗搪塞，打发 If you say that you are palmed off with a lie or an excuse, you are annoyed because you are told something in order to stop you asking any more questions. 
  【语法信息】：be V-ed P with n
  【搭配模式】：usu passive
  【语用信息】：disapproval
  【语域标签】：mainly BRIT 主英

Mark was palmed off with a series of excuses.马克被一连串借口打发了。
