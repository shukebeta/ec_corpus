He has staked his political future on an election victory...他把自己的政治前途押在竞选获胜上。
He has staked his reputation on the outcome.他将自己的名声赌在了这个结果上。

4.N-COUNT	可数名词  利害关系;重大利益;(企业等中的)股本，股份 If you have a stake in something such as a business, it matters to you, for example because you own part of it or because its success or failure will affect you. 
  【搭配模式】：N in n

He was eager to return to a more entrepreneurial role in which he had a big financial stake in his own efforts...他渴望回到一个更多参与创业的角色，那样他所付出的努力会与自己的经济利益息息相关。
Detectives now believe the Mafia also had a stake in the plot and killed him when it went wrong.侦探们现在相信黑手党在这个阴谋中也有利害关系，并在事败后杀了他。

5.N-PLURAL	复数名词  比赛;竞赛 You can use stakes to refer to something that is like a contest. For example, you can refer to the choosing of a leader as the leadership stakes . 
  【搭配模式】：the supp N

Britain lags behind in the European childcare stakes.英国在儿童保育方面落后于欧洲其他国家。

6.N-COUNT	可数名词  桩 A stake is a pointed wooden post which is pushed into the ground, for example in order to support a young tree. 

7.PHRASE	短语  提出权利要求;主张权利 If you stake a claim, you say that something is yours or that you have a right to it. 
  【搭配模式】：V inflects

Jane is determined to stake her claim as an actress...简决心主张自己作为一名女演员的权利。
Baguet's success staked his claim for a place in Belgium's world championship team.巴古埃特的成功使他有资格成为比利时世界锦标赛参赛队伍中的一员。相关词组：stake out
