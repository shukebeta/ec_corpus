Her heart beat so fast and she was full to the brim with joy.她心跳加快，满心喜悦。相关词组：brim over

★☆☆   brimful

1.ADJ	形容词  充满…的 Someone who is brimful of an emotion or quality feels or seems full of it. An object or place that is brimful of something is full of it. 
  【搭配模式】：v-link ADJ of/with n

She was brimful of energy and enthusiasm...她精力充沛，热情洋溢。
The United States is brimful with highly paid doctors.美国有很多薪酬很高的医生。
