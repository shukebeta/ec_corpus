★☆☆   crisscross

1.VERB	动词  在…交叉往返;纵横交错于 If a person or thing criss-crosses an area, they travel from one side to the other and back again many times, following different routes. If a number of things criss-cross an area, they cross it, and cross over each other. 
  【语法信息】：V n

They criss-crossed the country by bus...他们乘公共汽车游历这个国家。
Telephone wires criss-cross the street.这条街上电话线纵横交错。

2.V-RECIP	相互动词  (两组线或事物)相互交叉，相互交错 If two sets of lines or things criss-cross, they cross over each other. 
  【语法信息】：pl-n V
  【语法信息】：V pron-recip
  【语法信息】：V-ing
  【语法信息】：V-ed
  【语法信息】：Also V n

Wires criss-cross between the tops of the poles, forming a grid...电线杆顶端的电线相互交错，形成电线网。
The roads here are quite a maze, criss-crossing one another in a fashion that at times defies logic.这里的道路如迷宫般纵横交错，有时让人摸不着头脑。
...a complicated labyrinth of criss-crossing paths...迷宫般错综复杂的小径Support rows of beans with criss-crossed canes.用交错的小竹竿架起一排排的豆荚。

3.ADJ	形容词  (图案或设计)纵横交错的，十字交叉的 A criss-cross pattern or design consists of lines crossing each other. 
  【搭配模式】：ADJ n

Slash the tops of the loaves with a sharp serrated knife in a criss-cross pattern.把烤面包顶部用锋利的锯齿刀交错切开。
