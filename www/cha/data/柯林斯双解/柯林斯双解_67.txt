  【搭配模式】：det ADJ

3.PREP	介词  如果有（机会等） Given is used when indicating a possible situation in which someone has the opportunity or ability to do something. For example, given the chance means 'if I had the chance'.

Given patience, successful breeding of this species can be achieved.只要有耐心，是能成功培育出这一品种的。

4.PHR-CONJ-SUBORD	  考虑到；鉴于 If you say given that something is the case, you mean taking that fact into account.
