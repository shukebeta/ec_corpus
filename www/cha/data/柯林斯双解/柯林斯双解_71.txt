1.N-UNCOUNT	不可数名词  万圣节前夕（10月31日晚，传说此时可见鬼巫。当晚儿童常化装成鬼巫尽情玩闹） Halloween is the night of the 31st of October and is traditionally said to be the time when ghosts and witches can be seen. On Halloween, children often dress up as ghosts and witches.

★☆☆   hallucinate

1.VERB	动词  （因生病或吸毒而）产生幻觉 If you hallucinate, you see things that are not really there, either because you are ill or because you have taken a drug. 
  【语法信息】：V
  【语法信息】：V n
  【语法信息】：Also V that

Hunger made him hallucinate.饥饿使他产生了幻觉。
If you stared long enough and hard, you could even begin to hallucinate the appearance of small islands.如果长时间目不转睛地盯着看，你甚至会产生幻觉，看到一些小岛的出现。
