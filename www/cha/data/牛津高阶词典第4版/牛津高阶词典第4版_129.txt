 [Tn] damage the thread of (a screw) or the teeth of (a gear), esp by misuse 损坏（螺钉等）的螺纹或（齿轮）的齿（尤因使用不当）.

★☆☆   stripe


 / straIp; straIp/ n 

 long narrow band (usu of the same width throughout its length) on a surface that is usu different from it in colour, material or texture 条纹, 线条（通常全长等宽, 而颜色﹑ 材料或质地通常异於底面）: 
• a white table-cloth with red stripes 有红色条纹的白桌布 
• the tiger's stripes 老虎的斑纹 
• The plates have a blue stripe round the edge. 这些盘子边缘上有一道蓝线. →illus at pattern 见pattern插图.

• How many stripes are there on a sergeant's sleeve? 中士的袖子上有几道V形条纹? 
• She was awarded another stripe. 她又升了一级. 

 (usu pl 通常作复数) (arch 古) blow with a whip; stroke 鞭打; 抽打.
