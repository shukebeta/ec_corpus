os·mo·sis / ɔzˈməusis; ŋæmə ɑ:zˈmou- / noun əu] 
1. (biology 生 or chemistry 化) the gradual passing of a liquid through a membrane (= a thin layer of material) as a result of there being different amounts of dissolved substances on either side of the membrane   透析；渗透:
   Water passes into the roots of a plant by osmosis.   水经透析进入植物根部。 
2. the gradual process of learning or being influenced by sth, as a result of being in close contact with it  耳濡目染；潜移默化
• os·mot·ic / ɔzˈmɔtik; ŋæmə ɑ:zˈmɑ:tik / adj.:
   osmotic pressure   渗透压力
