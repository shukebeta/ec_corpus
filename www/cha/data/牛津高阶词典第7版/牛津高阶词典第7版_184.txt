  ▪ [V] 
   The rebel soldiers were forced to surrender.   叛军被迫投降。 
  ▪ [VN]

   He agreed to surrender all claims to the property.   他同意放弃对那笔财产的一切权利要求。

 PHRASAL VERBS

   He finally surrendered to his craving for drugs.   他最终克制不住,吸起毒来。 noun [U, sing.]
