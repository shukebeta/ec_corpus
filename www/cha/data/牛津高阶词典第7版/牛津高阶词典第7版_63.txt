   (formal) great respect and admiration; a good opinion of sb  尊重；敬重；好评:
   She is held in high esteem by her colleagues.  她深受同事们的敬重。 
   Please accept this small gift as a token of our esteem.   小小礼物,聊表敬意,请笑纳。 
⇨ see also self-esteem verb (formal) (not used in the progressive tenses 不用于进行时) 
1. [VN] [usually passive] to respect and admire sb / sth very much  尊重；敬重:
   a highly esteemed scientist  深受敬重的科学家 
2. [VN-N] (old-fashioned, formal) to think of sb / sth in a particular way  把…看作；认为:
   She was esteemed the perfect novelist.   她被认为是最完美的小说家。
